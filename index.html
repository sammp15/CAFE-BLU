About Us

Café Blu is a nice place you can come and relax with our furry friends. It showcases the cats in a personal and positive setting, compared to generic pet stores. While eating your food we will showcase 2-3 cats every 2 hours. We take stray cats(only healthy) to help benefits the communities stray cat problems. It provides a shelter for cats that no longer have a home. We also allowed people to adopt our wonderful cats and we do our best to provide them with a safe home. To see which cat suits you the best we highly recommend making an appointment for the Cat playroom to meet all of them.
